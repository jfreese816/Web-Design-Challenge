Weather Stats

This project utilizes a dataset of over 500 cities from OpenWeatherMap API to analyze a variety of weather metrics across the world.

The compiled data is fed into MatPlotlib to produce various visualizations by latitudinal position. Some of the metrics assesed were: temperature, humidity, cloud cover, and wind speed. This page compliles the source data and visuals resulting from the analysis.
